WHO WE ARE

HGEC Educational Consultancy Ltd is one of the leading educational consultancy and travel agency companies in Nigeria. With several years of offering indelible services to our students and clients, ensuring they achieve their overseas education and travel abroad goal.

Our aim is to make international education accessible for everyone and to bridge the gap of fear of securing visas between our clients and their countries of choice. This will do with our utmost best and this has been bringing our various recognitions across diverse nationalities and countries.

At HGEC Educational Consultancy we are all about helping you achieve your dream of studying abroad.
